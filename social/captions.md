## Posting cadence recommendation

**Don't post all 12 on day 1.** Facebook flags burst-posting on new pages as inauthentic.

| Day | Page A post | Page B post |
|-----|-------------|-------------|
| 1   | 01 (hero)   | 01 (hero) — 3h later |
| 2   | 02          | 02 |
| 3   | 03          | 03 |
| 4   | 06 (viral)  | 06 |
| 5   | 04          | 04 |
| 7   | 07 (social proof) | 07 |
| 9   | 08 (price)  | 08 |
| 11  | 09          | 09 |
| 13  | 10          | 10 |
| 15  | 05          | 05 |
| 17  | 11          | 11 |
| 19  | 12 (CTA)    | 12 |

12 posts over ~3 weeks per page = healthy cadence, won't trigger spam flags.
